About

Born in Ahmedabad, India, Maharshi moved to Edmonton at seventeen as an international student. Navigating cultural transition, language development, and academic adjustment without local support shaped a commitment to adaptability and steady performance under pressure. The loss of his grandfather, a lawyer who inspired his early interest in the profession, deepened his resolve to pursue legal education and serve newcomers and underrepresented communities.

After completing a diploma and entering the workforce full time, he returned to finish his bachelor’s degree, earning a 3.9 GPA in his final two years while holding internships and professional roles. His long-term interests are corporate and commercial law, real estate, and related transactional practice areas.

Academics & Legal Focus

Maharshi’s academic focus includes contracts, constitutional law, property, torts, and criminal law. He maintains a rigorous personal knowledge system with structured case tables, exam frameworks, and analytical outlines to support doctrinal precision and exam readiness.
